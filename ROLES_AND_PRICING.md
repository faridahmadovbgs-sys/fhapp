---

### 3. Member - $0.99 per transaction
**Basic user access within an organization.**

#### Characteristics:
- Invited into an Account (or multiple Accounts)
- Limited permissions based on the account's permission model
- May view or interact with assigned resources
- Pay-per-transaction model ($0.99 per transaction)

#### Access Level:
- View-only or limited interaction rights
- Cannot manage other users
- Cannot create sub-profiles
- Access determined by Account Owner or Sub-Account Owner

## Use Cases

### Small Business Example:
- **Account Owner**: Business owner ($49.99/month)
- **Sub-Account Owner**: Department manager (1st FREE)
- **Members**: Employees ($0.99 per transaction each)
- **Sub-Profiles**: Multiple business locations (FREE)

### Family Account Example:
- **Account Owner**: Parent ($49.99/month)
- **Sub-Account Owner**: Spouse (FREE)
- **Members**: Extended family members ($0.99 per transaction)
- **Sub-Profiles**: Children's profiles (FREE)

### Enterprise Example:
- **Account Owner**: CEO ($49.99/month)
- **Sub-Account Owners**: 
  - CFO (FREE)
  - CTO ($9.99/month)
  - COO ($9.99/month)
- **Members**: Various employees ($0.99 per transaction each)
- **Sub-Profiles**: Different departments/projects (FREE)

## Billing Notes

1. **Account Owner**: Charged monthly regardless of activity
2. **Sub-Account Owner**: First one is FREE, additional charged monthly
3. **Members**: Only charged when they perform transactions ($0.99 each)
4. **Sub-Profiles**: No charge, unlimited creation
5. **Multi-Organization**: Users can be different roles in different organizations, each billed separately

---

## Getting Started

1. **Register** as a user (free)
2. **Upgrade to Account Owner** ($49.99/month) to create your organization
3. **Invite Sub-Account Owners** (1st free, additional $9.99/month)
4. **Invite Members** ($0.99 per transaction)
5. **Create Sub-Profiles** (free, unlimited)
